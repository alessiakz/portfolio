About Me

Hi! My name is Alessia Kettlitz and I'm an up and coming data analyst, with a focus on health-related issues. I hold a Bachelor's Degree in Science from the University of Toronto and a Master's Degree in Public Health from Emory University.

Having lived across seven major metropolitan cities, spaning three continents, I've gained a unique perspective that has made me an expert at navigating cross-cultural environments. Through these experiences, I've learnt the importance of understanding the context of the data we use to develop sustainable and accurate solutions to problems.

Data doesn't live in a void, and we shouldn't treat it like it does!

I want to help you solve your problems through data-driven and contextually-informed solutions!

I'd love to get connected: message me on LinkedIn or send me an email!
